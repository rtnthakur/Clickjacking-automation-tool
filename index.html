Clickjacking (UI Redress Attack) occurs when an attacker uses multiple transparent or opaque layers (iframes) to trick a user into clicking on a button or link on another page when they intended to click on the top-level page.


This bypasses CSRF tokens because the request is initiated by the user's legitimate click action.

  • Download malware silently.
  • Capture keystrokes (if combined with other attacks).
